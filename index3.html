Stroke Distribution for Writer Identification of Indic Scripts

Research

Proposed a novel method of stroke extraction and clustering from offline handwritten documents. Created an alphabet of strokes and used their distribution as new features for identification in Telugu and Kannada. Acheived 97% accuracy on a Telugu database of 150 writers and 100% accuracy on a Kannada database of 54 writers. Paper accepted at ACPR 2017.
